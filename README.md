## About Me

I'm a final-year **BSc Informatics & Computer Science** student at Strathmore University, Nairobi. I build AI-powered systems, full-stack web platforms, and mobile applications — always grounded in real-world impact.

Certified in **Data Analytics & Visualization** (iLab Africa, 2024). I have 10+ projects shipped across Python, Kotlin, React, and Next.js, with hands-on experience in RAG pipelines, REST APIs, and cloud automation.

Beyond the screen, I captain the **Strathmore University Tennis Team** and actively participate in praise and worship.

- Interned as IT Support at **Missions of Hope International (MOHI)** — Jan to Mar 2026
- Built a **RAG chatbot (Rafiki IT)** for MOHI staff, enabling 24/7 AI-powered IT support
- Leading an **AI chatbot initiative** for Kenya's Public Service Commission covering architecture, data governance under the Data Protection Act (2019), and citizen-centric design
- Developing my **FYP** — Kenya Airways crew ground transport optimizer using Genetic Algorithms, Random Forest, and offline-first Android
- Open to internships, collaborations, and remote opportunities

## Featured Projects

### Rafiki IT — MOHI Support Chatbot
> Intelligent RAG-powered IT support assistant for a non-profit organization

Built with **Python, FastAPI, LangChain, and a vector database** during my IT internship at Missions of Hope International. Rafiki (Swahili for "Friend") enables MOHI staff to get empathetic, mission-aligned IT answers 24/7 — replacing manual support overhead with conversational AI.

`Python` `FastAPI` `LangChain` `RAG` `Vector DB` `Next.js`

**[View Repository](https://github.com/EthanBwibo/mohi-chatbot)**

### Hilton the Artist — Bespoke Artist Portfolio
> Full-stack artist portfolio and commission management platform

Built with **Next.js, Supabase, and Framer Motion** — features immersive galleries, fluid animations, secure authentication, and RLS-protected database access. Deployed on Vercel.

`TypeScript` `Next.js` `Supabase` `Framer Motion` `Tailwind CSS`

**[View Repository](https://github.com/EthanBwibo/Hilton-the-Artist)**
